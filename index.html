My Successful Projects

#1 Automatic Sun Tracking System

An Automatic Sun Tracking System is a device designed to continuously orient a solar panel toward the sun to maximize the amount of solar energy captured throughout the day. Unlike fixed solar panels, which remain stationary, a sun tracker adjusts its position in real time, significantly improving efficiency. This project uses an Arduino Uno as the central controller to automate the tracking process. The system typically employs Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) as light sensors and servo motors (or DC motors with a motor driver) to adjust the solar panel’s orientation

Gadgets:- (2)Solar Pannel, Arduino Uno Board,5V Power Source For Arduino Uno Board,Light Motion Sensor,Servo Motor,Small Led Bulbs,Thermacol,12v battery, Dc Cooling Fan
